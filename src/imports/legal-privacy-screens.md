#mobile_app

Task: Design the Legal & Compliance screens for the MemoryGame creator app.

Context:
The app must provide standard legal and privacy controls for users. These screens should be simple, readable, and mobile-friendly.

Screens to create:

1. Terms of Service
2. Privacy Policy
3. Cookie Consent
4. Data Export / Delete Account

All screens should follow the same visual style as the rest of the app.

------------------------------------------------

TERMS OF SERVICE SCREEN

Header:
Back button
Title: "Términos de servicio"

Content layout:

Scrollable content area with sections.

Each section includes:
Section title
Paragraph text

Example sections:
- Introducción
- Uso del servicio
- Responsabilidad del usuario
- Propiedad del contenido
- Limitación de responsabilidad
- Contacto

Text should be easy to read with:
- comfortable line spacing
- clear section separation

Footer:
"Última actualización: Marzo 2026"

------------------------------------------------

PRIVACY POLICY SCREEN

Header:
Back button
Title: "Política de privacidad"

Content sections:

- Qué datos recopilamos
- Cómo usamos los datos
- Cookies
- Servicios de terceros
- Derechos del usuario
- Contacto

Content should be scrollable and formatted like a standard policy page.

Footer:
"Última actualización: Marzo 2026"

------------------------------------------------

COOKIE CONSENT MODAL

This appears the first time a user visits the app.

Modal layout:

Title:
"Usamos cookies"

Description:
"Usamos cookies para mejorar la experiencia y analizar el uso del producto."

Buttons:

Primary:
"Aceptar todas"

Secondary:
"Solo necesarias"

Text link:
"Más información"

Behavior:
- Modal appears at the bottom or center of the screen
- Accepting stores consent
- Link opens the Privacy Policy

------------------------------------------------

DATA EXPORT / DELETE ACCOUNT SCREEN

Header:
Back button
Title:
"Privacidad y datos"

Sections:

Export Data

Description:
"Descarga una copia de tus datos."

Button:
"Solicitar exportación"

Delete Account

Warning text:
"Eliminar tu cuenta borrará todos tus juegos y datos."

------------------------------------------------

Design constraints:

- mobile-first layout
- consistent typography with existing screens
- readable long-form text
- comfortable vertical spacing
- avoid heavy UI elements
